EXPERIENCE

This video is about my participation in activities on Malaysia's 60th independence day at Dataran Merdeka, Kuala Lumpur. I and 29 people represented Klang to participate in the national level performance and we were given 60 days off to focus on training to make the performance a success and were given an allowance of 300 Malaysian ringgit. Various memories and experiences and getting to know many talented and interesting people. I hope to participate in activities like this in the future.
